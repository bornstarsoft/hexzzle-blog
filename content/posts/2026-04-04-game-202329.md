Get ready for a wild ride as the upcoming game 'Good Luck' promises to redefine urban survival gaming. Set to launch on April 8, 2026, for PC, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a chaotic city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, its designed to deliver a unique blend of humor and high-stakes gameplay.

Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, 'Good Luck' stands out with its unpredictable physics and lack of checkpoints, making every playthrough a test of skill and patience. Whether you're dodging banana peels or navigating through trash cans, the game guarantees a chaotic experience that will keep players on their toes. As it gears up for its PC release, fans are eagerly anticipating its arrival on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S later this year.

With its engaging mechanics and emphasis on cooperative play, 'Good Luck' is set to become a favorite among gamers looking for a fresh challenge. Will you be ready to tackle the urban jungle when it launches?
