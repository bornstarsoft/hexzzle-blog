After nearly four decades, the beloved action platformer series Impossible Mission is set to make a triumphant return with its third installment. Originally launched on the Commodore 64 in 1984, this iconic series has captivated gamers with its unique blend of action and strategy, and the upcoming release promises to build on that legacy with exciting new mechanics.

Developed by Icon 64 and published by Psytronik, Impossible Mission 3 will allow players to infiltrate the lair of the nefarious Dr. Elvin Atombender while dodging his menacing security robots. This new chapter introduces innovative gameplay features, including an EMP gun and the ability to destroy damaged scenery, enhancing the classic experience for both new and returning players.

Scheduled for digital release on May 18, 2026, alongside physical editions, this game not only appeals to nostalgic fans but also invites a new generation to experience the thrill of retro gaming. Will Impossible Mission 3 reignite the spark of classic gaming in today's digital landscape? Let's find out!
