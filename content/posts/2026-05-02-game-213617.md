PC gamers, rejoice! If you're in search of a delightful way to unwind this weekend, look no further than the free demo of Nested, an enchanting puzzle game that promises to captivate with its colorful aesthetic and clever challenges. Developed by Nathan Sioui as part of the GMTK 2024 Game Jam, Nested has already garnered attention as one of the standout winners, showcasing a unique gameplay experience that revolves around stacking nesting dolls in creative ways.

The core mechanics of Nested invite players to solve puzzles by matching the right sizes and colors of nesting dolls, offering a cozy yet stimulating environment for puzzle enthusiasts. With interactive elements like Buttons that alter your stack's properties and Swap Nodes that allow you to switch dolls, the game presents endless possibilities for exploration and creativity. Whether you're pushing, entering, or even destroying dolls, each level is designed to keep you engaged and entertained.

If you enjoy indie games that blend relaxation with a touch of challenge, Nested is definitely worth checking out. The demo is available now on Steam, and while the full game is set to launch in August 2026, the anticipation is already building.
