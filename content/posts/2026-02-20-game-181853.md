Exciting news for puzzle enthusiasts! The critically acclaimed game Opus Magnum is making its way to the Nintendo Switch with a Complete Edition, set to launch on March 17, 2026. This edition not only includes the original game but also features the prequel, De Re Metallica, promising a wealth of new puzzles and gameplay mechanics that will challenge both new and returning players.

Opus Magnum invites players to master the art of alchemy through intricate machinery and open-ended puzzles. As you design and build machines to create everything from remedies to weapons, youll find yourself immersed in a rich narrative filled with intrigue and danger. With its unique gameplay and compelling story, this title is sure to captivate a global audience of tech-savvy gamers.

Are you ready to dive into the world of alchemical engineering and unravel the mysteries of House Van Tassen? The Complete Edition is available for pre-order now at a discounted price, so mark your calendars for this must-have addition to your Nintendo Switch library!
