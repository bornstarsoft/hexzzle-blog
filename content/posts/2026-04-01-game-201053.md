In a significant development for the gaming industry, SHIFT UP has announced its acquisition of 100 percent of Japanese game developer Unbound Inc. This strategic move not only internalizes Unbound's talented development staff but also expands SHIFT UP's pipeline for PC and console games. With Unbound's team led by the renowned Shinji Mikami, known for his work on iconic titles like Biohazard and Devil May Cry, this acquisition is poised to enhance SHIFT UP's creative capabilities and market presence.

The integration of Unbound into SHIFT UP marks a pivotal moment for both companies. By bringing in a team with a rich history of successful game development, SHIFT UP is set to bolster its portfolio and deliver innovative gaming experiences to players worldwide. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, this acquisition could signal a new era of collaboration and creativity in game development.

As we look ahead, the question remains: how will this acquisition shape the future of SHIFT UP and the games they produce? With such a talented team now under their wing, the possibilities are endless.
