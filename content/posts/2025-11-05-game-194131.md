November is shaping up to be an exciting month for Xbox Game Pass subscribers, with Microsoft announcing a lineup of 11 new titles, including the highly anticipated Call of Duty: Black Ops 7. This addition is sure to thrill gamers who are eager to dive into the latest installment of the iconic franchise without any extra cost, provided they are Game Pass Ultimate or PC Game Pass members.

The new titles will be rolling out in the first half of November, featuring a mix of genres and gameplay styles. From the action-packed Dead Static Drive to the strategic Sniper Elite Resistance, theres something for everyone. However, its important to note that six games will be leaving the service on November 15, so players should make sure to experience those before they disappear.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, Xbox Game Pass remains a strong contender in providing value to gamers. With such a diverse lineup this month, it raises the question: what other surprises does Microsoft have in store for the future of Game Pass?
