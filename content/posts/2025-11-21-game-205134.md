The 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards showcased the best in gaming, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 dominating the night by winning all seven awards it was nominated for. This year, over 21 million votes were cast, highlighting the immense passion and engagement of the gaming community. The awards not only recognize outstanding games but also celebrate the creativity and innovation that drive the industry forward.

Among the notable winners, Ghost of Yotei took home the award for Best Console Game, while Hollow Knight: Silksong was recognized as the PC Game of the Year. The event also featured exciting categories like the Streamers' Choice Award, which went to Peak, reflecting the growing influence of streaming culture on gaming. As we look ahead, the Golden Joystick Awards remind us of the vibrant and evolving landscape of gaming, leaving fans eager for whats next.
