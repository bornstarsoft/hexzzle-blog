In a significant move to broaden its global gaming footprint, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has amassed over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to create a diverse gaming ecosystem, leveraging EverMerge's established success in Western markets to introduce it to new audiences, particularly in high-growth regions like India.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights. The company aims to enhance EverMerge's appeal through culturally relevant storytelling and innovative engagement strategies. With a strong player community already in place, JetSynthesys is committed to investing in the game to ensure it continues to evolve and captivate users.
