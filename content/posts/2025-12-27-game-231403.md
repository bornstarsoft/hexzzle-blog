Epic Games Store has just revealed its 10th free mystery game, and it's the intriguing co-op puzzle game, We Were Here Together. Available for a limited time starting December 26, 2025, at 9:00 AM PST, this game promises a unique experience that emphasizes teamwork and communication between players. If you enjoy solving puzzles with a partner, this is an opportunity you won't want to miss!

In We Were Here Together, players take on the roles of two explorers navigating the frozen Antarctic landscape in search of a sky flare. The game is designed around voice communication, requiring players to describe their surroundings and share clues to solve intricate puzzles. With no action elements, the focus is solely on collaboration and strategic thinking, making it a perfect choice for those who thrive on teamwork.

To claim your free copy, simply visit the Epic Games Store between December 26 and December 27, click "Get" on the game, and it will be added to your library permanently. As the Epic Games Store continues its holiday tradition of offering free games, its exciting to see what other titles will be revealed. Will you be diving into this co-op adventure with a friend?
