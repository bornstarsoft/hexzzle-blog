The gaming world is buzzing with excitement as HAOPLAY announces the upcoming launch of Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster, a casual puzzle game that promises to redefine mobile gaming. Set to release on iOS and Android later this year, players will step into the webbed feet of Captain Quack, leading a flock of avian allies on a quest to reclaim their home from monstrous foes. With pre-registration now open, gamers can look forward to a treasure trove of rewards upon the game's official launch.

In Quack Quack Attack, players will engage in a unique pachinko-style gameplay experience, strategically managing a team of birds to eliminate enemies and clear stages. With 50 unique birds across six classes, the game encourages players to experiment with different combinations, enhancing their strategy and gameplay. The ability to evolve bird stats and customize abilities adds depth, ensuring that each playthrough feels fresh and engaging.

Beyond the main campaign, players can also challenge themselves in the Purgatory Tower, unwind at the Fishing Ground, or compete against others in the Starry Expedition mode. As the anticipation builds for its release, one can't help but wonder how Quack Quack Attack will shape the future of mobile puzzle games. Will it become the next must-have title on your device?
