In an unexpected twist, Taito's quest to locate its first-ever claw machine, the Crown 602, has led to the discovery of its successor, the Crown 603. This search, which garnered over 4,400 tips from the Japanese public, highlights the enduring nostalgia and passion for retro gaming in Japan. Despite not finding the elusive Crown 602, Taito's journey has unearthed a piece of gaming history that many enthusiasts may have overlooked.

The Crown 603, while in rough condition, represents a significant link to the past, showcasing the evolution of arcade games in Japan. Taito's commitment to restoring this machine reflects the company's dedication to preserving gaming heritage. As they work to potentially revive the Crown 603, it raises an intriguing question: how many other forgotten gems are still waiting to be rediscovered in the world of retro gaming?
