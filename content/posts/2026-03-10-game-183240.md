American McGee has announced that his upcoming game, based on the Plushie Dreadfuls line, will serve as a spiritual successor to his iconic Alice series, connecting its narrative threads to the end of Alice: Madness Returns. This exciting development comes after years of uncertainty regarding the future of the Alice franchise, particularly after EA's refusal to greenlight a third installment.

In a recent interview, McGee emphasized that the new game will not only honor the legacy of Alice but also provide closure for fans who have long awaited a continuation of her story. By linking the protagonist James' journey to Alice's, McGee aims to create a narrative that resonates with both new players and longtime fans alike, all while navigating the legal complexities of the Alice IP.

As McGee ventures into this new chapter, it raises questions about the evolution of beloved franchises in the gaming industry. Will this spiritual successor capture the dark, whimsical essence that made Alice a cult classic? Only time will tell, but fans are certainly eager to see how this story unfolds.
