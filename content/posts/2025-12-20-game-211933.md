In a world where pet enrichment can often come with a hefty price tag, a recent TikTok hack has caught the attention of cat owners everywhere. TikToker Sparkles and Sunshine (@sparklesandsunshineblog) has demonstrated how to turn simple, empty toilet paper rolls into an engaging puzzle game for cats, providing both entertainment and mental stimulation without breaking the bank.

The process is refreshingly straightforward: all you need are a few empty toilet paper rolls, some ribbon, cat treats, and a hole puncher. By punching holes in the rolls and folding them to create treat pockets, you can easily hang this DIY toy between two chairs. Your feline friends will love batting at the tubes to release their favorite treats, making it a win-win for both pets and their owners.

This innovative approach not only saves moneycommercial versions of similar toys can cost anywhere from $20 to $40but also encourages creativity in pet care. With the ability to customize the game using materials you already have at home, its a sustainable solution that keeps our furry companions active and engaged.
