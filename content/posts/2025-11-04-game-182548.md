In an exciting turn of events for gaming enthusiasts, both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the impressive launch titles for the Nintendo Switch 2 but also showcases the innovative gameplay and engaging experiences that Nintendo continues to deliver.

As the gaming community eagerly anticipates the results, these nominations reflect a broader trend in the industry where quality and creativity are being celebrated. With titles like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei also in the running, the competition is fierce. Fans can vote for their favorites, making this an exciting time for gamers to engage with their beloved franchises.

With the awards ceremony set for November 21, 2025, the anticipation is palpable. Will Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza take home the coveted title? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain: Nintendo is once again at the forefront of gaming excellence.
