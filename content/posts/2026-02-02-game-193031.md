If you're a PlayStation Plus subscriber, there's a hidden gem you need to check out: The Exit 8, a short yet captivating horror puzzle game that has recently made its way to the service. This indie title, developed by Kotake Create, offers a unique first-person experience where players navigate a looping Japanese metro tunnel, identifying eerie anomalies that challenge both your perception and nerves.

What sets The Exit 8 apart is its blend of subtle and overt creepiness, making it a perfect fit for those who enjoy a psychological thrill without the full-blown horror. With gameplay that encourages you to spot discrepancies in the environment, each loop becomes a test of your attention to detail. Completing the game takes just a couple of hours, and it features easy trophies, making it accessible for casual gamers looking for a quick yet engaging experience.

As the gaming community continues to explore indie titles, The Exit 8 stands out as a must-play for anyone with a PlayStation Plus subscription. Will you take the plunge into this unsettling yet intriguing puzzle game?
